iPhone 17 Air

Future goal

2025 · iOS · Ultra‑thin flagship

Thin, light and modern – exactly the kind of phone that’s scary and exciting to repair. This is one of my dream devices to work on once I’ve mastered the older iPhones.

Ultra thin Lightweight frame Future repair target

Quick profile

  • Modern edge‑to‑edge display with very slim bezels.
  • Lightweight design, which usually means fragile frame + more glue.
  • High‑end cameras and battery packed into a thin body.

Repairs I’ve done / plan to do

  • Future: screen replacement on a donor/broken 17 Air.
  • Future: battery replacement while trying not to twist the frame.
  • Study: how Apple balances thinness, cooling and repairability.

What I learned

Once I actually work on a 17 Air, this section will track my mistakes, wins and tips for next time – especially any tricks for handling the thin frame and glued‑down parts safely.
